2024 VexU World Championships

A spectacular way to cap off an amazing season. After a strong 7-1-1 performance in qualifications, we got knocked out in the quarterfinals eliminations. Nonetheless we are proud of our performance netting us a Think Award and being crowned the Skills World Champions at 460 points.
